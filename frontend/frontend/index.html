Welcome to Calcoin

Calcoin is money that works for you, for everyone else, and for the people. Calcoin uses algorithms to increase and decrease the velocity of money. In recession, Calcoin increases the velocity of money by paying you for transactions, while during economic booms Calcoin makes transactions more expensive to prevent runaway upward movement.

But why is calcoin usefull?

The problem with current day financial architecture is that it abuses these natural cycles of the economy, short term incentives make politicians and regulators attempt to pursue upward economic growth at all costs, to help stabilize the value of the currency and the financial market, we created Cal Coin. This allows people to convert their tokens to a medium of exchange that is allowed to expand and contract to maintain inflation.
